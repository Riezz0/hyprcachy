لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللّٰهُ - There is no god but Allah
سُبْحَانَ اللّٰهِ - Allah is free from imperfection
اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ - All praise be to Allah
اَللّٰهُ أَكْبَرُ - Allah is the Greatest
لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللّٰهِ - There is no power (in averting evil) or strength (in attaining good) except through Allah
سُبْحَانَ اللهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ - Allah is free from imperfection and all praise is due to Him
سُبْحَانَ اللهِ الْعَظِيْم - Allah is free from imperfection, the Greatest
يَا ذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالْإِكْرَامِ - O The Lord of Majesty & Honour
يَا حَيُّ يَا قَيُّوْمُ ، بِرَحْمَتِكَ أَسْتَغِيْثُ - O The Ever Living, The One Who sustains and protects all that exists; I seek assistance through Your Mercy
